Why have I been invited?

Recruitment for the MS Register is being carried out at a number of Neurology centers across the UK. All people with a confirmed diagnosis of MS who attend your neurology clinic are being invited to join the Register.

What is the purpose of the study?

The purpose of the MS Register is to capture better, higher quality information about living with MS in the UK today. More data needs to be gathered about the physical, environmental and social effects for people and carers affected by the condition.
